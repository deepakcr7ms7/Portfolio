System Context

Internal inventory and ordering platform supporting replenishment and throughput during peak demand. Shifted from reactive ordering to a proactive, data-informed decision flow used in production.

In production Data-driven policy Throughput-focused

Before

  • Reactive ordering (order when run out)
  • No prioritization across items
  • Stockouts during peaks; excess otherwise
  • Decisions driven by intuition

After

  • Proactive ordering policy with recommendations
  • Priority-based replenishment
  • Safety stock aligned to variability and cost
  • Explainable logic for adoption

Key Engineering Decisions

Priority-based ordering

Ranked items by variability, cost, and operational criticality.

Statistical forecasting

Used trend + variance + demand signals (simple, reliable, explainable).

Safety stock policy

Calibrated buffers by priority to reduce stockouts without overbuying.

Workflow integration

Recommendations embedded directly into ordering—no separate “ML tool”.

Product & UX Evolution

Guided ordering UX

Turned “guess and order” into a clear recommendation + reasoning flow.

Operational dashboards

Surfaced demand spikes, risk items, and exceptions to reduce firefighting.

Faster decision cycles

Reduced ambiguity by consolidating signals into one place and one policy.
